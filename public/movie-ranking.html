The Best Movies According to Tommy

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

Tells a realistic story of love with futuristic (yet believable) technology aspects.

Seven Psychopaths

I can't find a more smooth dialouge and script with the most impressive casting and dynamic acting.

Little Miss Sunshine

Black comedy at its finest, the most disfuntional family with some of the worst luck are stuck together to realise all they need is each other

The lord of the rings: The fellowship of the ring

My favourite of the trilogy. Such an adventure. Nuff said
